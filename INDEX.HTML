MARYLAND

Things To Do   Places To Visit   Maryland Cities  

Maryland is a state that borders Virginia, West Virginia, Washington D.C and Pennysylvania. Maryland was named after Queen Henrietta Maria of England. It is found in the Mid-atlantic region of the United States. The governor of Maryland is Wes Moore of the Democratic Party. Baltimore is the largest city in the state of Maryland. The capital city of Maryland is Annapolis. The largest county in Maryland is Montgomery County.
